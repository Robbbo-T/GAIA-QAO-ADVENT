```

- Rt = Revenue in year t
- Ct = Operating cost in year t  
- Mt = Maintenance cost in year t
- r = Discount rate (7%)
- I0 = Initial investment
- t = Year (0 to 50)

Quantum Advantage:
- Mt reduced by 40% through predictive maintenance
- Service life extended from 30 to 50 years
```
